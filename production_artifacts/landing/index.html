How it works

Simple. Automatic.
Impossible to ignore.

Three steps stand between you and healthier screen habits.

1

Set your schedule

Tell Pawzy how long you want to work and how long your breaks should be. Adjust anytime from the tray menu.

2

Your companion arrives

When your work session ends, your chosen companion slides dramatically onto your screen. Breaks are enforced — no sneaking past.

3

Break. Return. Repeat.

Step away, stretch, hydrate. When the timer ends, your companion disappears and you're back to work, refreshed.

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Pawzy is intentionally minimal. No subscriptions, no accounts, no data collection. Just you and your companion.

🖥️

Full-screen enforcement

Breaks can't be dismissed or skipped. The overlay is always-on-top.

🎨

4 companions

Choose from Neko Cat, Shiba Inu, Chill Panda, or Fox Kit.

⚙️

Custom timers

Any work duration from 5–120 min. Any break from 1–30 min.

🚀

Launch on login

Toggle auto-start so Pawzy is always running in the background.

🪶

Lightweight

Single AppImage, ~150MB installed. No Python needed on your machine.

🔒

100% local

No telemetry, no servers. Everything lives on your machine.
