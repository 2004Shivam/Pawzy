How it works

Simple. Automatic.
Impossible to ignore.

1

Set your schedule

Tell Pawzy how long you want to work and how long breaks should be. Adjust anytime from the system tray menu.

2

Your companion arrives

When your session ends, your chosen companion slides dramatically onto your screen. Fullscreen, inescapable — no sneaking past.

3

Break. Return. Repeat.

Step away, stretch, hydrate. When the timer ends your companion disappears and you're back — refreshed and ready.
