How it works

Simple. Automatic. Impossible to ignore. 🚫

Three steps stand between you and healthier screen habits.

1

Set your schedule

Tell Pawzy how long you want to work and how long your breaks should be. Adjust anytime from the system tray.

2

Your companion arrives

When your work session ends, your companion slides dramatically onto your screen. The overlay is fullscreen and inescapable.

3

Break. Return. Repeat.

Step away, stretch, hydrate. When the timer ends, your companion disappears and you're back — refreshed and ready.
